A little about me

I'm a Senior Software Developer with 10+ years of experience, more specifically working with C#, Java, SQL and most recently Microservices (Java w/ Kubernetes) and MS Azure DevOps Continuous Integration and Development. In the course of my career, I have created web and mobile applications to help optimize business operations and bring companies closer to their clients.

Nowadays, I design code and debug application features in different platforms. I also act as a Performance Mentor, coaching Developers to improve their development skills.

In one of my personal projects, I'm working on the development of a tool to control the PC completely remotely. Another one is the development of test controls system for drones using Spring Boot Hibernate, REST. Another is a tool aimed at enhancing character recognition using Python and Tensorflow.

In my research career, I have had the opportunity to conduct interviews with clients to determine usability of several deployed solutions to customers, and to identify what keeps the business in the market.

In my downtime, you'll see me playing volleyball or brushing up on culinary skills.

I'm passionate about delivering cutting-edge deliverables that improve the overall user experience and satisfaction and brings new innovation, making positive impacts on people's lives.
